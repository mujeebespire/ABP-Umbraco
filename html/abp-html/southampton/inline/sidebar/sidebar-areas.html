Areas

  • Horizon Cruise Terminal

    Serving major cruise lines, including MSC Cruises, Norwegian Cruise Line, P&O Cruises and Disney Cruises.
  • City Cruise Terminal

    Opened in 2003 and was upgraded in in 2007. It is one of the busiest terminals, designed to accommodate the largest ships.
  • Mayflower Cruise Terminal

    Serving major cruise lines, including MSC Cruises, Norwegian Cruise Line, P&O Cruises and Disney Cruises.
  • QEII Cruise Terminal

    The Queen Elizabeth II (QEII) Cruise Terminal opened in 1966 and was modernised in 2003 and 2016.
  • Ocean Cruise Terminal

    The Ocean Cruise Terminal opened in September 2009 and is close to the Old Town.
  • Western Docks

    Home to three Cruise Terminals: Mayflower, Horizon and City.
  • Western Extension

    The Southampton docks extension, built entirely on reclaimed land was opened in 1968.
  • Eastern Docks

    The Eastern Docks is home to the QEII and Ocean Cruise Terminals, the grain terminal and the automotive terminal.
  • Eling Wharf

    Eling Wharf is a 41 acre site, which sits adjacent to the A35 and is predominantly used for storage.
  • Marchwood Ind. Park

    Marchwood Industrial Park is a well-established industrial site and home to more than 60 businesses.

  • Solent Gateway

    ABP Solent Gateway are the operators of Marchwood Military Port. The 83 hectare port supports MOD operations, and commercial automotive and bulk operations.
  • Strategic Land Reserve

    The Land Reserve comprises 500 acres of land. A large part of which was reclaimed using arisings from various dredging operations in the Solent area.
