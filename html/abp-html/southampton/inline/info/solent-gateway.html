Solent Gateway

ABP Solent Gateway Limited (SGL), the operator of Marchwood Port, has been part of ABP Southampton since 2023. Traditionally a military port owned by the Ministry of Defence (MoD), Marchwood Port is operated under a 35-year lease held by ABP Solent Gateway, covering a 200 acre site.

ABP SGL supports military and commercial port operations in various sectors, including aggregates, automotive, heavy plant export, break bulk, project cargoes, container storage, repair and maintenance, and renewable energy. As a multi-modal site, ABP SGL is one of the premier locations for importing and exporting cargo, boasting excellent sea, rail, and road connectivity.
