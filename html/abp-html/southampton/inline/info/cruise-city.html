Dock Gate 10 Parking Info

City Cruise Terminal

City Cruise Terminal opened in 2003 and serves major cruise lines including Royal Caribbean and Celebrity Cruise Lines.

City Cruise Terminal is easily accessible from A33, West Quay Road, please follow signs for Dock Gate 10. Once through Dock Gate 10, take a left and the terminal will be on the right-hand side of the road. On cruise days, the terminal can also be accessed through Dock Gate 8.

By train, the closest station is Southampton Central and is a 10 minute walk from the station to the terminal. Alternatively, taxis can be found either side of the train station.

The nearest airport is Southampton. While taxis are the quickest route to and from the port. The closest train station to the airport is Southampton Airport Parkway, and trains run frequently from Southampton Central to Southampton Airport Parkway.

Arrival We encourage passengers to arrive at their allocated arrival time. There is an area for passenger drop off and a short stay car park. The short stay car park is about a 5 minute walk to the terminal and is closed to the Dock Gate 10 port entrance. Please note, if passengers wish to park in the long stay car park, this must be pre-booked with parking provider ABParking.
