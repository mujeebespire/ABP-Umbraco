Horizon Cruise Terminal

The Horizon Cruise Terminal opened in 2021. It is the newest cruise terminal with state-of-the-art green infrastructure including 2,000 roof-mounted solar panels and clean energy shore power for cruise ships. Using shore-side power means cruise ships can turn off their engines in port to achieve zero emissions.

Horizon Cruise Terminal serves major cruise lines, including MSC Cruises, Norwegian Cruise Line and Disney Cruises. Additionally, it caters to luxury cruise lines such as Regent Seven Seas Cruises and Oceania Cruises.

Horizon Cruise Terminal is easily accessible from A33, West Quay Road, please follow signs for Dock Gate 10. Once through Dock Gate 10, the terminal is directly in front of you, take a right to reach the terminal.

Horizon Cruise Terminal is a 10 minute walk from the train station.
