Dock Gate 4 Parking Info

Ocean Cruise Terminal

The Ocean Cruise Terminal opened in September 2009 and is close to the Old Town. This terminal serves several major cruise lines, including P&O Cruises, Cunard and Princess Cruises.

The terminal is easily accessible from the A33, Platform Road, please head for Dock Gate 4.

By car, please follow the signs for Dock Gate 4, you will go through a manned security gate, Ocean Cruise Terminal is very close to the entrance, and you can access it by turning right at the first roundabout once inside the Port. Short stay parking is located just behind the coach bays, to your right as you face the terminal. There is also long stay parking available on the port, this will need to be booked prior to your arrival.

It is a 30 minute walk from the train station.
